About Us

BTA-Lab (BuildTheAlpha Laboratory) is a research laboratory focused on the intersection of machine learning and quantitative finance. We are dedicated to applying cutting-edge artificial intelligence technologies to financial market analysis, risk management, and investment strategy optimization, with the aim of building next-generation intelligent financial decision systems.

Our research team consists of researchers with backgrounds in computer science, mathematics, statistics, and finance, working together to explore innovative applications of artificial intelligence in the financial domain.

Recent Publications

OPDR: Order-Preserving Dimension Reduction for Semantic Embedding of Multimodal Scientific Data
Gong, C., Shen, G., Guo, L., Tallent, N., & Zhao, D. (2024)
arXiv preprint arXiv:2408.10264
Collaboration with: HPDIC Lab and Pacific Northwest National Laboratory (PNNL)

This work proposes to reduce the dimensionality of output embedding vectors such that the set of top-k nearest neighbors do not change in the lower-dimensional space, namely Order-Preserving Dimension Reduction (OPDR).
